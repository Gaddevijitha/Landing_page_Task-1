Welcome to Nellore Specials

This Webpage is about Nellore and its specialities

Nellore, a city located in the state of Andhra Pradesh, India. Renowned for its rich cultural heritage and unique culinary delights. The city is known for its spicy and flavorful cuisine. Nellore specials include dishes like Nellore Chepala Pulusu (fish curry), Prawns Fry, and traditional sweets (malai kaja). The region is also famous for its temples and scenic beauty, making it a must-visit destination for food lovers and travelers alike.
